# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 13, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 13, 2026

Skill Editor ("the App") is an open-source, browser-based editor for agent skill files. It is maintained by Milos Milovanovic.

This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data You Create in the App

All files, edits, and session data you create in the App are stored **exclusively in your browser's local storage** (IndexedDB). This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is never accessible to us or any third party
- Is deleted when you clear your browser data

**We do not operate a backend, database, or user account system.** There is nothing to "sign up" for and no personal information is collected through the App itself.

---

## 2. Hosting and Server Logs

The App is hosted on **Vercel**. When you visit the site, Vercel may automatically collect standard server log data, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and version (user-agent)
- Pages visited and timestamps
- Referring URL

This data is collected and processed by Vercel under their own [Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy). We do not control or have direct access to raw Vercel server logs.

---

## 3. Analytics

The App uses **Google Analytics** to understand general usage patterns (e.g., page views, session duration, device types). Google Analytics may set cookies on your browser and collect:

- Anonymized IP address
- Device and browser information
- Pages visited and navigation flow
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

Google Analytics data is used solely to improve the App. We do not use it to identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics by:

- Using a browser extension such as [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings
- Using a content blocker that filters analytics scripts

For details on how Google processes this data, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 4. Cookies

The App itself does not set any cookies. However, **Google Analytics** may set cookies (e.g., `_ga`, `_gid`) to distinguish unique visitors and track sessions. These are third-party cookies governed by Google's policies.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties. The only data processing that occurs is through the hosting and analytics services listed above.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 8. Your Rights

### For EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- **Access** any personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing of your data
- **Object** to processing, including analytics
- **Data portability**

Since the App stores all user-created data locally in your browser, you already have full control over it. For any requests related to server logs or analytics data, contact us at the address below.

### For California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell any data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

---

## 9. Data Retention

- **Browser data (IndexedDB):** Retained until you clear it. We have no access to it.
- **Server logs (Vercel):** Retained per Vercel's data retention policies.
- **Analytics data (Google Analytics):** Retained per Google's default retention settings (typically 14 months).

---

## 10. Security

Since all user content stays in your browser, there is no server-side data to breach. For the hosting and analytics layers, we rely on the security practices of Vercel and Google respectively.
